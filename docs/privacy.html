Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-16

PokeStop Buddy is designed to work entirely on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Your submission drafts are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to us or any third party. The app contains no analytics or tracking in v1.0.

Data you create

Submission drafts you create live in the app's local storage. Deleting the app, or using “Delete all submissions” in Settings, removes this data.

Third-party services

None are integrated in v1.0. If this changes, this page and the App Store privacy labels will be updated before release.
