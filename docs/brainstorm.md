# Brainstorm (non-authoritative)

> Ideas only. Nothing here is a contract. Promote to `specs/` when rules lock.

## Vision

A fast, convenient iOS companion for people who submit PokeStops / Wayspots via
Niantic Wayfarer. Help them **generate** strong submission content and **iterate /
fine-tune** it until it's review-ready.

## Core jobs-to-be-done

- "I found a candidate — quickly draft a good title/description/supporting statement."
- "My submission keeps getting rejected — help me improve it."
- "Let me keep a list of candidates and their drafts."

## v1.0 (locked → specs)

- Offline template content generator (title/description/supporting).
- Quality coach scoring + actionable tips.
- Submission editor with version history (iterate/fine-tune, revert).
- Submission library (list/detail).
- Settings + legal links.

## Later (FutureIdeas/)

- Photo composition guidance + EXIF/location checks.

- Map view of candidates; location capture.

- iCloud sync, widgets, App Intents ("New PokeStop draft").
