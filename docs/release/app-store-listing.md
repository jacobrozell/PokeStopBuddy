## Description

```
Waypoint Writer helps Pokémon GO players draft stronger Wayfarer nominations — before you open the in-game form.

Describe a real-world place, generate a title, description, and supporting statement, then fine-tune until the Quality coach says you're ready. Copy everything into Pokémon GO when you're at the location.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Create and save submission drafts on your device
• Generate nomination text from place name, category, key features, and eligibility
• Choose a writing style: concise, descriptive, or formal
• Get live quality scoring with blockers, warnings, and tips
• Edit manually and keep version history — revert anytime
• Copy all fields or share with one tap
• Browse a built-in submission guide (process, fields, categories, eligibility, photos, copy workflow)
• Works on iPhone and iPad, portrait and landscape
• Light and dark mode

BUILT FOR PRIVACY
• Works fully offline — no account, no cloud sync
• Your drafts stay on your device
• No analytics or tracking in v1.0

HOW IT FITS YOUR WORKFLOW
1. Scout a candidate location
2. Draft in Waypoint Writer at home or on the go
3. Visit the site, take photos in Pokémon GO
4. Paste your polished text into the official nomination form

Waypoint Writer does not submit nominations for you and does not connect to your Niantic or Pokémon GO account. You always complete photos, pin placement, and upload inside the official game.

NOT AFFILIATED
Waypoint Writer is an independent tool. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Niantic, Inc., The Pokémon Company, or Nintendo.
```

## App Review Information

### Demo account

Not required — no login, no server, no paywall.

### Notes for reviewer

```
Waypoint Writer is an offline drafting tool for Pokémon GO / Niantic Wayfarer nomination text.

No login or network is required for core features. To test:
1. Launch the app (skip onboarding if desired via normal UI, or complete the short tour).
2. Tap + on Submissions.
3. Enter a place name (e.g. "Town Square Fountain"), pick a category, select at least one eligibility pillar.
4. Tap "Generate content".
5. Review Quality coach panel; tap "Copy all".

Settings → Submission guide opens in-app help. Settings → Privacy policy / Support open Safari to GitHub Pages.

The app does not submit to Niantic, access Pokémon GO APIs, or collect user data. Not affiliated with Niantic or The Pokémon Company.
```

Thanks for testing Waypoint Writer!

Please try:
1. First launch — complete or skip onboarding; confirm defaults in Settings stick.
2. New submission — fill place name, category, eligibility → Generate content.
3. Quality coach — confirm blockers/warnings make sense; tap an issue to jump to the field.
4. Save, relaunch — draft should persist in the library.
5. Copy all — paste into Notes and confirm all three fields copied.
6. Submission guide — open from Settings or library empty state; browse at least two topics.
7. iPad (if available) — split library/detail and landscape editor layout.
8. Accessibility — VoiceOver on editor + library; Dynamic Type at largest size.

What we need from you:
• Bugs (steps to reproduce)
• Confusing labels or missing guidance
• Quality coach false positives/negatives
• Generated text that sounds unhelpful or policy-risky
• Screenshot or screen recording for layout issues

Send feedback via TestFlight’s “Send Beta Feedback” or email listed on the TestFlight page.
