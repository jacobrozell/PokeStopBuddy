Need help with Waypoint Writer?

Common questions

  • How do I create a submission? Tap + on the Submissions screen, fill in the place details, and tap “Generate content”.
  • How do I improve a draft? Check the Quality coach panel — it lists blockers, warnings, and tips. Edit fields or change inputs and regenerate.
  • Where is my data? Everything is stored locally on your device.

Contact

Open an issue at the project repository, or email the developer listed on the App Store page.
